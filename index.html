Celebrating the Well-Curated Life

Paul and Sophie Yanacoupoulos-Gross are introducing their first US home furnishings store in Brooklyn Heights.

The carefully curated collections that Paul and Sophie offer mix muted tones and natural materials in designed objects sourced from throughout the world.

The collections include beds, tables and benches in a full range of sizes, upholstered seating, chairs and lighting, rugs, basketry, bed linens, cushions, crockery, and glassware.

Paul and Sophie’s very special style is reflected in every aspect of one of New York’s most exciting new home furnishings stores.
